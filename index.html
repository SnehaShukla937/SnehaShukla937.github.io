Welcome..

Hello everyone! I am a PhD research scholar in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Indore, India. I have recently submitted my PhD thesis and am currently seeking postdoctoral or research opportunities. My research focuses on developing robust, trustworthy, and high-performing deep learning-based medical imaging models under the guidance of Dr. Puneet Gupta. Our aim is to improve the reliability and security of healthcare systems by offering interpretable and explainable AI solutions for life-critical medical applications. My areas of interest include Medical Imaging, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Adversarial Robustness, and Explainable AI. I have over 3 years of experience working with Python and PyTorch frameworks, including advanced data analytics. If you find any common interests or potential collaborations, please feel free to connect. To know more about my research journey, kindly visit the About page.
