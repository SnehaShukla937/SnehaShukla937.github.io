Welcome

Hello! I am a PhD research scholar in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Indore, India. Currently, I am devising robust, trustworthy and better-performing deep learning-based medical imaging models under the guidance of Dr. Puneet Gupta. Our aim is to improve the reliability and security of healthcare systems, offering interpretable and explainable AI solutions to life-critical healthcare applications. My area of interests revolve around Medical Imaging, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Adversarial Robustness, and Explainable AI. I possess over 3 years of experience working on Python and PyTorch frameworks, including advanced data analytics. To know about my research journey, kindly visit this Link.
